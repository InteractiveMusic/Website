Current Instrument Line

I have developed five musical controllers following traditional forms. They are the WindBoard, MelodiKeyZ, DrumKit, Shaker, and the Syntar.

WindBoard

An expressive wind controller for breath-based play.

MelodiKeyZ

A force-sensing keyboard merging acoustic feel with electronic control.

DrumKit

A dynamic drum interface for rhythmic creativity.

Shaker

A handheld shaker with touch-responsive triggers.

Syntar

A modern synthesizer guitar merging strings and force sensing.
